### Ingredients

- 2 Cups Oats
- 2.5 Bananas
- 1/2 Cup of chocolate chips

### Method

- Mash the banana and mix with oats and chocolate chips in a bowl. 
- Mould into 12 cookies. 
- Bake in over at 180 degrees for 10 minutes.
